Saleshandy is one of the newer players in the cold email space, and they've grown fast by offering unlimited email accounts on every plan — a direct shot at Instantly's model.

Their pricing looks attractive at first glance. But dig into the prospect limits and add-on costs, and the picture changes.

## Pricing: Monthly vs One-Time

Saleshandy's plans are based on prospect (contact) limits:

| Plan | Monthly Price | Prospects | Emails/Month |
|------|--------------|-----------|--------------|
| Outreach Starter | $25 | 2,000 | 10,000 |
| Outreach Pro | $74 | 30,000 | 125,000 |
| Outreach Scale | $149 | 60,000 | 250,000 |
| Outreach Scale Plus | $219 | 100,000 | 350,000 |

All plans include unlimited email accounts and unlimited sender rotation. That's good.

But the prospect limits are the catch. An agency managing 10 clients with 5,000 prospects each needs the Scale plan at minimum — $149/month = **$1,788/year**.

**Cleanmails:** $497 one-time. No prospect limits. No email limits. No monthly fees.

Break-even vs Saleshandy Outreach Pro: **6.7 months**. After that, pure savings.

## Where Saleshandy Wins

**White-label agency portal.** Saleshandy's agency features let you create branded client dashboards. Good for agencies that want to show clients their campaign performance without revealing the tool behind it.

**Built-in warm-up (TrulyInbox).** Included in all plans. No separate tool needed.

**Lead finder add-on.** Their B2B database lets you find prospects by company, title, and industry directly within the platform.

**Unified inbox.** All replies from all email accounts in one view. Makes managing high-volume responses easier.

**Lower barrier to entry.** $25/month to start is accessible for solo senders testing cold email for the first time.

## Where Cleanmails Wins

**No prospect limits.** Saleshandy's biggest limitation. If you're an agency loading 10,000+ prospects per client, you're forced into expensive plans. Cleanmails has zero limits on contacts.

**No email volume caps.** Saleshandy caps emails per month (10,000–350,000 depending on plan). Cleanmails has no sending limits — your only constraint is your infrastructure capacity.

**Inbuilt SMTP.** Saleshandy requires you to connect external email accounts (Gmail, Outlook, SMTP). Cleanmails has its own SMTP engine — you control the entire sending stack.

**Unlimited email validation.** Saleshandy charges credits for verification. Cleanmails includes full SMTP handshake validation at no extra cost. For agencies processing large lists, this is a significant saving.

**One-time cost.** No monthly drain. No annual renewals. No price increases. Pay once, use forever.

**Self-hosted = full control.** Your data, your server, your rules. No dependency on Saleshandy's uptime, terms of service, or pricing decisions.

## Who Should Use Saleshandy

- Solo senders or small teams just starting with cold email ($25/month is low-risk)
- Agencies that need white-label client portals
- Teams that want a built-in lead finder alongside their sending tool
- Users who prefer fully managed SaaS with zero infrastructure responsibility
