Lemlist is one of the most popular cold email tools on the market. It's well-designed, has solid deliverability features, and their multi-channel sequences are genuinely useful.

But it's also expensive — especially if you're an agency or a team.

Let's break down exactly where Lemlist wins, where Cleanmails wins, and who should use what.

## Pricing: The Biggest Difference

Lemlist uses per-seat pricing. Every user on your team needs their own subscription.

| Plan | Lemlist (per user/month) | Annual Cost (3 users) | Annual Cost (5 users) |
|------|--------------------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Email Starter | $39 | $1,404 | $2,340 |
| Email Pro | $69 | $2,484 | $4,140 |
| Multichannel Expert | $99 | $3,564 | $5,940 |

**Cleanmails:** $497 one-time. Unlimited users. Unlimited everything. No per-seat fees, no monthly charges.

For a 3-person team on Lemlist's Email Pro plan, you break even with Cleanmails in **3.6 months**. After that, you're saving $207/month — every month, forever.

## Where Lemlist Wins

**Multi-channel sequences.** If you need LinkedIn + email + phone in one sequence, Lemlist does this natively. Cleanmails is email-only.

**Personalized images.** Lemlist's dynamic image personalization (custom screenshots, LinkedIn previews in emails) is unique. No other tool does this as well.

**Built-in warm-up.** lemwarm is included in every plan. With Cleanmails, you'll need a separate warm-up tool ($15–29/month).

**Easier onboarding.** Lemlist is a hosted SaaS — sign up and start. Cleanmails requires a VPS and initial setup (though free installation is included).

## Where Cleanmails Wins

**Cost at scale.** The moment you have 2+ team members or 3+ clients, Cleanmails is dramatically cheaper. A 5-person agency saves $4,000–5,000/year.

**Unlimited everything.** No contact limits, no email limits, no sender limits, no workspace limits. Lemlist gates features behind plan tiers.

**Built-in email validation.** Lemlist charges credits for validation. Cleanmails includes full SMTP handshake validation — unlimited, no per-lead fees. This alone saves agencies $200–500/month compared to standalone validation tools.

**Inbuilt SMTP.** Cleanmails ships with its own SMTP engine. You don't need to buy and configure external sending infrastructure. Lemlist requires you to connect Gmail, Outlook, or a third-party SMTP.

**Data ownership.** Your campaigns, contacts, analytics, and sending history live on your server. You own it. If Lemlist changes their terms, raises prices, or goes down — your data goes with them.

**No vendor lock-in.** With Cleanmails, you can export everything, move servers, or modify the setup. With Lemlist, you're renting access month to month.

## Who Should Use Lemlist

Lemlist is the better choice if you:

- Need LinkedIn + email multi-channel sequences
- Want personalized image embeds in your emails
- Are a solo sender doing fewer than 200 emails/day
- Don't want to manage any infrastructure
- Value plug-and-play over ownership

## Who Should Use Cleanmails

Cleanmails is the better choice if you:

- Run an agency with multiple clients
- Have a team of 2+ people doing outreach
- Send more than 500 emails/day
- Want to own your infrastructure and data
- Need unlimited email validation without per-credit fees
- Want to stop paying monthly for something you could own

## The Migration Path

Switching from Lemlist to Cleanmails is straightforward:

1. Export your contact lists from Lemlist as CSV
2. Purchase Cleanmails ($497) and get your license key
3. Provision a VPS — Hetzner CX21 ($5/month) works perfectly
4. Run the one-command installer (free setup assistance included)
5. Import your contacts and recreate your sequences
6. Set up a warm-up tool (Mailreach or Warmbox, $15–29/month)

Total ongoing cost: $5–30/month for VPS + warm-up. Compare that to $69–99/month per user on Lemlist.

## The Verdict

Lemlist is a polished, feature-rich tool that excels at multi-channel outreach and personalization. If those features are critical to your workflow, it's worth the monthly cost.

But if your primary use case is **cold email at scale** — sending thousands of emails across multiple domains and clients — Cleanmails gives you more for less. Dramatically less.

The math is simple: Cleanmails pays for itself in 2–5 months depending on your team size. After that, every month is pure savings.
